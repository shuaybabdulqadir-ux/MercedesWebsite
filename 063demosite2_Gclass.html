The G Class

The fascination of the Geländewagen.


Redefining boundaries.

No mountain too high or river too deep for the electric G-Class. Now with up to 850 mm wading depth, it outperforms previous models, ensuring a steadfast ride in the toughest conditions. With electrification, the off-road capabilities of the G 580 with EQ Technology diversify even further.

Peak off-road performance.

Powered by intelligent systems, the G 580 with EQ Technology demonstrates outstanding off-road capabilities. From improved maneuverability with G-STEERING, to intelligent off-road crawl function, to the G-TURN enabling an up to 720-degree turn on the spot.

Freedom for every adventure.

New Driver Assistance Systems, the digital off-road experience, elevated on-board comfort and the powerful sound experience G-ROAR offer a perfect balance of off-road performance and on-road ease – no matter the destination or terrain.



Exterior


An icon of unique.

MANUFAKTUR offers almost limitless design options to customise the G 580 with EQ Technology. Additionally, according to your wishes, from a more classic look with radiator grille and spare wheel on the rear, to expressive electric aesthetics with the black panel grille and striking design box.


Powerful proportions.

Upright lines, wide track, and a high ground clearance emphasize strength and authority, giving the G-Class a commanding stance.


Distinctive design details.

Circular LED headlamps, exposed door hinges, and the signature spare wheel at the rear celebrate its authentic, functional character.


Modern refinement

Clean surfaces, precision edges, and contemporary lighting elements blend rugged capability with luxury craftsmanship.



Interior


Advanced digital cockpit.

A wide dual-screen display integrates the instrument cluster and infotainment system, offering intuitive control and a modern driving experience.


Luxury meets toughness.

The interior blends high-quality materials like leather, metal, and wood with a solid, upright design that reflects the G-Class’s rugged DNA.


Exceptional comfort.

Ergonomically designed seats with premium upholstery provide outstanding comfort for both city driving and long journeys.
