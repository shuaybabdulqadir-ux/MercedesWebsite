Interior


AMBIENT LIGHTING PREMIUM PLUS

Unique atmosphere: The ambient lighting with precise, direct lines of light provides the right lighting scene for you in almost any situation with a total of 64 colours. In the 10 colour themes, the colours can also alternate spectacularly. You can adjust the brightness in 5 levels and 4 dimming zones.


CLIMATISED FRONT SEATS

Pleasant seat climate in all weathers: The seat ventilation system supplies the front seats with a large-area airflow, cools them down and thus also regulates moisture. In cooler weather, the seat heating quickly brings the seat surface and backrests up to a pleasant temperature.


THROUGH-LOADING FEATURE

Create space for new things: With the rear bench seat, which can be folded down electrically in a ratio of 1/3 to 2/3, you can transform the boot into a long loading area in no time at all. This makes transporting large or bulky items of luggage child's play.
